A NOTE TO THE MOTH WHO ATE MY SWEATER
I hope that you are feeling better now that you are full of sweater.
But, frankly, don't you think it's rude to use my clothes as common food?
I'd rather share my bread and rolls than deal with sweaters full of holes.
The next time you intend to dine on sweaters that are clearly mine,
I must insist -- call you to task --
don't take a bite unless you ask.
By now, I think you surely know
the answer is a big, loud,
NO!
